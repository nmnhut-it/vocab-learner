# Writing Prediction 24.11 to 31.12 by Real IELTS Exams

Source: Real IELTS Exams (realieltsexams.com)

## Question Types Summary
- Agree/Disagree: 18 questions
- Discuss Both Views: 14 questions
- Advantages/Disadvantages: 7 questions
- Two-Part Questions: 6 questions
- Causes/Solutions: 2 questions
- Positive/Negative Development: 3 questions

---

## Questions

### 1. Traditional Education vs Technology
**Many believe that the traditional educational system of teachers and students in a classroom will be replaced by 2050. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this view?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Education & Technology

### 2. Communication Technology & Social Relationships
**Some people think that modern communication technology is having a negative effect on social relationships. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Technology & Society

### 3. Private vs Public Healthcare
**Some people think keeping health is a basic need of humans, thus medical care should not be run by profit-making companies. Do the disadvantages of private health care outweigh the advantages?**
- Type: Advantages/Disadvantages
- Category: Health & Society

### 4. Children's Free Time Activities
**Some people say free-time activities for children should be organized by parents. Others say that children should be free to choose what they do in their free time. Discuss both views and give your opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Family & Children

### 5. International Cooperation Benefits
**Some people think that the main benefit of international cooperation is in the protection of the environment, while others believe that businesses mainly benefit. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Environment & Business

### 6. Local vs Foreign Films/TV
**Some people think that it is important for a country to develop their own films and TV programs instead of importing foreign ones. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Media & Culture

### 7. Government Spending on Youth Activities
**More government money should be spent on free-time activities for young people. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Government & Youth

### 8. Parenting Courses
**To improve children's lives, mothers and fathers should attend parenting and child development courses every year. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Family & Education

### 9. Foreign vs Local Films
**Many people prefer to watch foreign films rather than locally produced films. Why could this be? Should governments give more financial support to local film industries?**
- Type: Two-Part Question
- Category: Media & Culture

### 10. Wildlife Protection
**Many believe that it is important to protect all wild animals, while others think that it is important to protect some, not all of them. Discuss both views and give your opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Environment & Wildlife

### 11. Prison vs Community Service
**Some people think that all lawbreakers should be sent to prison, while others think there are better alternatives for those whose crimes are minor, such as making them do work for the local community. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Crime & Justice

### 12. Government Support for Artists
**Some believe that the government should support artists like musicians, painters, and poets, while others argue that this is a waste of money. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Arts & Government

### 13. Contacting Extraterrestrial Life
**Some scientists believe that intelligent life forms may exist on other planets and some want to send messages to contact them. Others think it is a bad idea because these life forms may be too dangerous. Discuss both views and give your opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Science & Space

### 14. Natural Resource Consumption
**World's natural resources such as oil, forests and fresh water are consumed at an ever-increasing rate. What are the causes of this? How can it be solved?**
- Type: Causes/Solutions
- Category: Environment & Resources

### 15. Government Funding for Health & Education
**Some people think that the government should pay for health care and education, but others believe it is not the government's responsibility. Discuss both views and give your opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Government & Society

### 16. Private vs Government Scientific Research
**In today's world, private companies, rather than the government, pay for and conduct most scientific research. Do you think the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?**
- Type: Advantages/Disadvantages
- Category: Science & Business

### 17. News Relevance
**Some people think the news has no connection to people's lives, so then it is a waste of time to read the news in the newspaper and watch television news programs. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Media & Society

### 18. Celebrities as Role Models
**In many parts of the world, some famous people are considered as 'role models' and they are having an increasing influence on the young. Is this a positive or negative development?**
- Type: Positive/Negative Development
- Category: Society & Youth

### 19. Vehicle-Free Days
**Some cities have vehicle-free days when private cars, trucks, and motorcycles are banned from the city center. People are encouraged to use public transportation such as buses, taxis, and the metro on vehicle-free days. Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?**
- Type: Advantages/Disadvantages
- Category: Environment & Transport

### 20. Having Children Later in Life
**In many countries, people decide to have children at a later age than in the past. Why? Do the advantages of this development outweigh the disadvantages?**
- Type: Two-Part Question (Why + Advantages/Disadvantages)
- Category: Family & Society

### 21. Academic Ability Grouping in Schools
**Some people think that schools should select students according to their academic abilities, while others believe that it is better to have students with different abilities studying together. Discuss both views and state your own opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Education

### 22. Urban Migration for Traffic Solutions
**The best way to solve traffic and transport problems is to encourage people from the suburbs or the countryside to live in the cities. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Urban Planning & Transport

### 23. Arts vs Science & Technology
**In today's world of advanced science and technology, we still greatly value our artists such as musicians, painters and writers. What can arts tell us about life that science and technology cannot?**
- Type: Direct Question
- Category: Arts & Society

### 24. Living Alone vs Extended Family
**Many people in cities live alone or in small family units instead of with extended family. Do you think this is a positive or negative development?**
- Type: Positive/Negative Development
- Category: Family & Society

### 25. Free 24/7 Public Transport
**The best way for a government to solve the problem of traffic congestion is to provide free public transport 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Transport & Government

### 26. Charity: Local vs International Aid
**Some people think that charity organizations should only help people in their own country. However, others believe these organizations should aid people in great need wherever they live. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Society & Ethics

### 27. Museums: Local vs Foreign Art
**Museums and art galleries should concentrate on local works rather than showing the cultures or artworks from other countries. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Culture & Arts

### 28. Food Self-Sufficiency
**Many people believe that countries should produce food for all the population and import as little as possible. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Economics & Agriculture

### 29. Paternity Leave
**All fathers should be entitled to take time off from work when their children are born. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Family & Work

### 30. Technology & Face-to-Face Communication
**The increasing use of mobile phones and computers makes people lose the ability to communicate face-to-face. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Technology & Society

### 31. Internet as Most Important Invention
**There have been many inventions in human history, such as the wheel. Some people think the most important thing is the internet. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this opinion?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Technology & History

### 32. Space Exploration vs Earth Problems
**Some people want governments to spend money looking for life on other planets. Others, however, think this is a waste of public money when there are so many problems on Earth. Discuss both views.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Science & Government

### 33. Museums & Local Visitors
**Museums and historical sites are mainly visited by tourists, not local people. Why is this the case? What can be done to encourage local people to visit these sites?**
- Type: Two-Part Question
- Category: Tourism & Culture

### 34. Sports as Big Business
**Sports have become a big business, with high-earning sportspeople and large amounts of money involved. Do you think this is a positive or negative development?**
- Type: Positive/Negative Development
- Category: Sports & Business

### 35. Peer Pressure
**Young people are often influenced in their behaviors by others in the same age group. Some argue that peer pressure is important, while others feel it has disadvantages. Do the disadvantages of peer pressure outweigh the advantages?**
- Type: Advantages/Disadvantages
- Category: Youth & Society

### 36. Western vs Traditional Clothing
**In many countries, people now wear Western-style dresses such as suits and jeans rather than traditional clothing. Why is this? Is this a positive or negative development?**
- Type: Two-Part Question
- Category: Culture & Globalization

### 37. Negative News in Media
**The tendency of news reports in the media to focus more on problems and emergencies than on positive developments is harmful to individuals and society as a whole. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Media & Society

### 38. Cosmetic Surgery
**An increasing number of people are choosing to have cosmetic surgery in order to improve their appearance. Why do people have operations to change the way they look? Do you think this is a positive or negative development?**
- Type: Two-Part Question
- Category: Health & Society

### 39. Success and Luck
**Whether or not a person achieves their aims in life is mostly related to luck. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Success & Life

### 40. Money as Work Motivation
**Many factors motivate people to work hard in the workplace. Some people think money is the most important. Do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Work & Motivation

### 41. Celebrity Privacy
**In some countries, celebrities complain about the way the media publicizes their private lives. Some people say that they should accept it as part of their fame. Do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Media & Privacy

### 42. Annual Driver Testing
**Some people think that the best way to improve road safety is to get drivers tested each year. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Transport & Safety

### 43. Fresh Water Usage Rights
**Some people think they have the right to use as much fresh water as they want, while others believe governments should tightly control the use of fresh water as it is a limited resource. Discuss both views and give your opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Environment & Resources

### 44. Celebrities: Fame vs Achievement
**Nowadays, celebrities are more famous for their glamour and wealth than for their achievements, and this sets a bad example for young people. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement?**

- Category: Society & Youth

### 45. Work and Social Life
**Some people think a job not only provides income but also a social life. Others think it is better to develop a social life with people you do not work with. Discuss both views and give your opinion.**
- Type: Discuss Both Views
- Category: Work & Social Life

### 46. Live Performances at Home
**These days, many people like to watch live performances (such as shows or concerts) at home, either on TV or on a computer, instead of attending in person. Do the advantages of this trend outweigh its disadvantages?**

- Category: Entertainment & Technology

### 47. Adult Illiteracy

- Type: Two-Part Question
- Category: Education & Society

### 48. Working Hours & Economic Success

- Type: Agree/Disagree
- Category: Work & Economics

### 49. Income Equality & Happiness

- Category: Society & Economics

### 50. Executive Salaries
