## Pattern of Issues Found

The most common issue was **formulaic sentence patterns**:
- "Governments can/must [verb] [noun]..." appeared frequently
- "AI systems can [verb]..." was repetitive
- Many examples followed the same structure

**Solution applied**: Varied sentence patterns to include:
- Different tenses (past, present continuous)
- Different subjects (not just "Governments")
- Specific details and numbers
- More natural, conversational academic English
